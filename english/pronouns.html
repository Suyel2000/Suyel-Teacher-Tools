What is a Pronoun?

⭐ Remember: A pronoun is a helper word that steps in to **take the place of a noun**! Instead of saying "Tim loves Tim's dog because the dog plays with Tim," we use stunt doubles to say "**He** loves **his** dog because **it** plays with **him**!"
