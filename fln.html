📊 FLN (Foundational Literacy & Numeracy)

🌱 Building Strong Learning Foundations

Foundational Literacy and Numeracy (FLN) is the cornerstone of a child’s learning journey. As emphasized in NEP 2020, every child must attain basic reading, writing, and mathematical skills by Grade 3 to ensure meaningful and lifelong learning.

This platform focuses on competency-based learning and experiential activities that help children understand concepts rather than memorize them. Through engaging tools, games, and visual experiences, learners develop confidence in both literacy and numeracy.

Our approach supports a holistic development of learners by integrating:

  • 📖 Reading, writing, and comprehension skills
  • 🔢 Number sense and mathematical reasoning
  • 🎯 Activity-based and joyful learning
  • 🧠 Critical thinking and problem-solving
