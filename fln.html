🌱 Why FLN Matters

Foundational Literacy and Numeracy (FLN) is the backbone of all future learning. As highlighted in NEP 2020, every child must achieve basic reading, writing, and mathematical skills by Grade 3 to ensure meaningful progress in education.

This platform promotes competency-based learning, where students understand concepts deeply through activity-based and experiential learning rather than memorization.

🔤 Writing & language development
🔢 Number sense & operations
🧠 Logical thinking & problem-solving
