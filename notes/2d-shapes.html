2D SHAPES

2D shapes are flat shapes. They have length and breadth but no thickness.
2D shapes are all around us! Look, identify and learn. 💡

Quick Facts

  • 2D shapes are flat and can be drawn on paper.
  • They have sides and corners (vertices).
  • Shapes can be different in size but not in shape.
