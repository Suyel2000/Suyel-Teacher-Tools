WHAT IS A CALENDAR?
  • A calendar is an organized chart that shows the days, weeks, and months of a specific year.
  • It allows us to keep precise track of timeline progressions and upcoming events.
  • Every calendar page maps one distinct month partition completely out.
Calendars help us manage our time smartly!

QUICK FACTS
  • There are exactly 7 days in 1 full week.
  • There are exactly 12 months in 1 full year.
  • A normal year contains 365 days altogether.
  • February contains 28 days normally, but counts 29 days during leap years!

WHAT IS A LEAP YEAR?
  • A leap year occurs once every 4 years and has 366 days instead of 365!
  • The extra day is added straight to the end of February, giving it 29 days.
  • To check if a year is a leap year, see if its number can be divided by 4 perfectly with no remainder.
  • Examples of leap years include 2024, 2028, and 2032!
