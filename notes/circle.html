Circle

A circle is a round, closed 2D shape
with no sides and no corners.
What is a Circle?
  • A circle is a round shape.
  • It is closed and curved.
  • It has no sides.
  • It has no corners (vertices).
  • All the points on the boundary are at the same distance from the centre.

Properties of a Circle
  • Sides: 0
  • Vertices (Corners): 0
  • Angles: 0
  • One continuous curved boundary.
  • Infinite lines of symmetry.

Quick Facts
  • A circle is a 2D shape.
  • It has no sides and no corners.
  • It has a curved boundary.
  • All points on the boundary are at the same distance from the centre.
  • A circle has infinite lines of symmetry.
  • It is not a polygon.
Important Terms
Centre (O) : The fixed point in the middle of the circle.
Radius (r) : The distance from the centre to any point on the circle.
Diameter (d) : A line passing through the centre and joining two points on the circle. It is twice the radius.
Different Positions of Points
Inside
Point lies inside the circle.
On the Circle
Point lies on the circle.
Outside
Point lies outside the circle.
Lines Related to a Circle
Secant
A line that cuts the circle at two points.
Tangent
A line that touches the circle at exactly one point.
Chord
A line segment whose endpoints are on the circle.
Comparing Circles
If r₁ < r₂, then the left circle is smaller than the right circle.
If r₁ = r₂, then both circles are equal in size.
