Division means "sharing or grouping equally".

It helps us find how many are in each group or how many groups we can make.

WHAT IS DIVISION?

  • ★ Division means splitting a number into equal parts.
  • ★ We use the division sign (÷) to divide numbers.
  • ★ It is one of the four basic operations in mathematics.
  • ★ It is used in everyday life.
Let's divide and make math easy and fun! ➗

QUICK FACTS 💡

  • ★ any number ÷ 1 = the number itself
  • ★ any number ÷ the number itself = 1
  • ★ 0 ÷ any number (except 0) = 0
  • ★ Any number ÷ 0 = Not possible
  • ★ Division is NOT commutative.
  • ★ Practice division to become fast and accurate!

WORD PROBLEMS – LET'S SOLVE!

1
There are 24 apples. If they are kept equally in 4 baskets, how many apples are in each basket?
Solution:
24 ÷ 4 = 6
Answer: 6 apples
2
A school has 315 students and 15 teachers. If students are divided equally, how many students per teacher?
Solution:
315 ÷ 15 = 21
Answer: 21 students
3
A ribbon is 96 cm long. If it is cut into pieces of 8 cm each, how many pieces can be made?
Solution:
96 ÷ 8 = 12
Answer: 12 pieces
4
There are 864 books. If arranged equally on 12 shelves, how many books on each shelf?
Solution:
864 ÷ 12 = 72
Answer: 72 books

MENTAL MATH TRICKS

★ ÷ 10 ➔ Drop one zero from the end
(e.g., 340 ÷ 10 = 34)
★ ÷ 100 ➔ Drop two zeros from the end
(e.g., 5700 ÷ 100 = 57)
★ ÷ 5 ➔ Multiply by 2, then divide by 10
(e.g., 65 ÷ 5 ➔ 130 ÷ 10 = 13)
★ ÷ 4 ➔ Halve the number twice
(e.g., 48 ÷ 4 ➔ 24 ➔ 12)
