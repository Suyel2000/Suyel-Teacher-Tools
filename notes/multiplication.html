WHAT IS MULTIPLICATION?

  • ★ Multiplication means adding the same number again and again.
  • ★ We use the multiplication sign (×) to multiply numbers.
  • ★ It helps us find the total quickly.
  • ★ It is one of the four basic operations in mathematics.
  • ★ It is used in everyday life.
Let's multiply and make math super easy! ❌
Example: 4 × 3 = 12
4 + 4 + 4 = 12 (3 times)
Here, 4 is the number being added repeatedly.

METHODS OF MULTIPLICATION

1

MULTIPLICATION WITHOUT REGROUPING

When the product of digits is a single digit.

Example: 23 × 3
TO
23
×3
69
Step-by-step:
• 3 × 3 = 9
(write 9 in ones place)
• 3 × 2 = 6
(write 6 in tens place)
So, 23 × 3 = 69
2

MULTIPLICATION WITH REGROUPING

When the product of digits is a two digit number.

Example: 47 × 6
⁴
HTO
47
×6
282
Step-by-step:
• 6 × 7 = 42
(write 2, carry 4)
• 6 × 4 = 24 + 4
(carry) = 28
So, 47 × 6 = 282
3

MORE THAN ONE DIGIT

Multiply the ones digit first, then tens, hundreds... and add.

Example: 132 × 24
ThHTO
132
×24
528(132×4)
2640(132×20)
3168(123×24)
Step-by-step:
1. 132 × 4 = 528
2. 132 × 20 = 2640
(add a zero for tens)
3. Add products:
528 + 2640 = 3168

WORD PROBLEMS – LET'S SOLVE!

1
A shop has 24 packs of pencils. Each pack has 15 pencils. How many pencils are there in all?
Solution:
24 × 15 = 360
Answer: 360 pencils
2
A school arranged 128 chairs in each of 18 classrooms. How many chairs are there in all?
Solution:
128 × 18 = 2,304
Answer: 2,304 chairs
3
A factory makes 345 toys in a day. It works for 27 days in a month. How many toys are made in a month?
Solution:
345 × 27 = 9,315
Answer: 9,315 toys

MENTAL MATH TRICKS

★ × 10 ➔ Add one zero (e.g., 34 × 10 = 340)
★ × 100 ➔ Add two zeros (e.g., 57 × 100 = 5700)
★ × 1000 ➔ Add three zeros (e.g., 8 × 1000 = 8000)
★ Double a number ➔ × 2  |  Triple ➔ × 3
★ Half of × 10 ➔ (n × 10) ÷ 2
e.g., 48 × 5 = (48 × 10) ÷ 2 = 240
